# Enterprise AI Governance Guardrails

Innovation ideas must not include secrets, credentials, or confidential data samples in prompts or working notes.

Required governance controls:
- documented business owner
- approved data access path
- privacy and sensitive-content review before pilot
- human oversight for customer-impacting decisions

Projects with strong business value may proceed only if the control gaps are captured explicitly in the remediation plan.
