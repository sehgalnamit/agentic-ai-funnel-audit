# Idea Intake Template

Use this template to submit an idea without self-scoring it.

## Business framing
- Idea title
- Sponsor / business owner
- Problem statement
- Target users or teams
- Business outcome expected
- KPI to move
- Why now

## Operating context
- Current process today
- Systems involved
- Required data sources
- Known dependencies
- Geographic or regulatory scope
- Delivery timeline expectation

## Technical concept
- Proposed workflow or user journey
- AI/automation pattern being considered
- Human review points
- Known risks or constraints
- Documents attached: business case, process note, architecture sketch

## What the platform will decide
- strategic alignment from strategy KB
- data readiness from data KB
- technical readiness from architecture and delivery KB
- market timing from trend and competitor KB
- governance posture from policy KB
- prerequisite investment before pilot
