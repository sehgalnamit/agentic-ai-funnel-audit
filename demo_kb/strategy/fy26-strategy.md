# FY26 Enterprise Strategy Priorities

The company is prioritizing AI programs that improve customer retention, increase service productivity, and reduce workflow friction in core operating teams.

Preferred ideas should:
- reuse existing platforms before buying new tools
- strengthen governed AI delivery rather than isolated experiments
- improve measurable KPI outcomes such as churn reduction, case resolution speed, and revenue protection
- create reusable data assets for future enterprise analytics

Ideas that require major new platforms without a clear KPI path should be deprioritized.
